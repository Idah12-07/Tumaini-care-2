## Danger Sign Thresholds (WHO/FIGO EPL Guidelines)

| Symptom | Threshold | Score | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heavy bleeding | >1 pad/hour or soaking | +3 | WHO EPL guidelines |
| Foul discharge | Any foul odour | +3 | FIGO EPL protocol |
| Right-sided pain | Unilateral severe | +3 | Ectopic warning sign |
| Fever | >38.5C or very hot | +2 | WHO fever threshold |
| Severe pain | Constant abdominal | +2 | General danger sign |
| Dizziness/faint | Reported weakness | +2 | Haemorrhage/shock sign |
| Vomiting | Repeated episodes | +1 | Secondary sign |
| Bleeding stopped | Completed miscarriage | -1 | Positive indicator |

## Risk Level Thresholds
- LOW: 0 points
- MODERATE: 1-2 points (CHW follow-up within 24h)
- HIGH: 3-4 points (CHW alert, home visit same day)
- EMERGENCY: 5+ points (Immediate hospital + CHW + referral)
